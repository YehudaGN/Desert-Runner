How To Play

Use the Spacebar or ArrowUp to jump over obstacles


Use LeftShift or ArrowDown to duck under obstacles


Ducking in midair will cause the player to land quicker


Click a character button to select that character


Click the Toggle Hitboxes button to display hitbox visualization


The Difficulty will dynamically increase


Make your selections before starting the game


Click Start to begin the game
