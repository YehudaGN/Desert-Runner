How To Play

Use the Spacebar to jump over obstacles


Use the LeftShift key to duck under obstacles


Ducking in midair will cause the player to land quicker


Press Esc to start the game


Click the Toggle Hitboxes button to display hitbox visualization


The Difficulty will dynamically increase
